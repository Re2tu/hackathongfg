What is a Sport? +

An activity involving physical exertion and skill in which an individual or team competes against another or others for entertainment.

Do Men and Women Ever Compete in the Same Sport? +

Firstly, some sports have mixed events. This is where men and women form a team (usually, but not always, a duo) and compete against another mixed team. This way, men and women compete together and against each other in a fair way as both teams feature the same number of male and female players.
